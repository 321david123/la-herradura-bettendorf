Who We Are

A real grill, tucked
inside The Plex.

La Herradura is a family-run, full-service Mexican restaurant and bar in Bettendorf, Iowa. Herradura means horseshoe in Spanish — and our golden horseshoe crest is a nod to good fortune and the warm hospitality you'll feel the moment you sit down.

We're not a concession stand. Our kitchen turns out generous, traditional plates: sizzling fajitas, sharing molcajetes, hand-made enchiladas and chimichangas — plus margaritas and ice-cold cervezas from a full bar. It's a four-and-a-half-star favorite — now with four locations: Bettendorf, Silvis, Coal Valley and a cantina up the river in Le Claire.
